Our Mission

To simplify accounting for businesses of all sizes with intuitive, powerful, and accessible software solutions.

To become the leading accounting platform in Bangladesh, empowering thousands of businesses with financial clarity.

Our Values

Integrity, innovation, and customer success drive everything we do at Khoium's Accounting.

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Khoium's Accounting began with a simple idea: make double-entry accounting accessible to everyone. What started as a small project has grown into a comprehensive accounting solution used by businesses across Bangladesh. Our software combines traditional accounting principles with modern technology to deliver accurate, real-time financial insights.
