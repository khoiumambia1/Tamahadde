frequently asked questions

Is the software free?

Yes! Khoium's Accounting is completely free to use with all features included.

Where is my data stored?

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. No data is sent to any server.

Can I export my data?

Yes, you can export transactions to CSV and download complete JSON backups.

Do I need internet?

Once loaded, the app works offline. Internet is only needed for initial load and maps.
